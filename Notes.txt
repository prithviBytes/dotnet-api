Access Specifiers:
    Public: Accessible for within and outside the class
    Protected: Within the class and any class that inherits it.
    Private: Accessible from within the class only.

Updating Migrations:
    Updating the model/schema of an entity without lossing the existing data.
    CMD: dotnet ef migrations add `MigrationName`
    To the update the DB
    CMD: dotnet ef database update.
